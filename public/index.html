Patent Title

User-worn device for noninvasive measurement of physiological parameters

Abstract Overview

A user-worn device configured to noninvasively measure a physiological parameter of a user, such as pulse rate and oxygen saturation. The wearable device includes a pulse oximetry sensor that transmits light of at least two wavelengths through tissue and detects the attenuated light to calculate blood oxygen levels.
